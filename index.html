Our Offers (65–70%)

When you submit a ticket, we check current fair market value (recent sold listings, major marketplaces, and demand) and respond with a cash offer.

Most sealed Pokémon TCG we buy lands in a 65–70% of fair market value payout range, depending on:
  • Condition of the seal and box
  • How in-demand the item is right now
  • Quantity and age of the product

If our offer hits your number, we'll finalize shipping or meetup details and send payment after verification.
